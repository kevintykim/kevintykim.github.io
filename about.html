About Me

My Story

I grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area and graduated from UC Santa Cruz with a Bachelor's Degree in Film and Digital Media. I started out with a number of analytical jobs for Silicon Valley's tech giants, before pursuing a more fulfilling career in user interface design.

My venture into the UI design field was inspired by my lifelong aptitude for digital media and design—as a child, I enjoyed playing around in Microsoft Paint, and in later years I often used Adobe Photoshop as a pastime. I hope design can grow into an even bigger part of my life, both as an occupation and as a creative outlet.

My other creative interests include writing, playing the keyboard, and web design. When I'm not creating, I enjoy watching movies and TV shows, reading books, and playing video games (though my imaginative side often conjures up its own ideas for games and shows—perhaps one day those ideas will see the light of day!).
